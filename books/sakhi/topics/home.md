## Thank you  

Thank you for taking the time to go through *Sakhi*. We hope this handbook has provided you with valuable insights and practical guidance as you prepare for one of life's most significant journeys. Whether you are planning for marriage, contemplating family life, or choosing to remain single, we trust that the knowledge shared here will empower you to make thoughtful and informed decisions.  

*Sakhi* is only the beginning. It is our deep wish to engage with marriage aspirants and, by Grace, aid them in any way we can through our diverse experiences. We encourage you to reflect on what you've learned and take the following steps before diving into the next section of topics:  

### Calls to Action  

1. **Reflect on Your Values and Goals**  
   Take some time to think about your personal values and long-term goals. How do they align with your vision for marriage or family life?  

2. **Discuss Key Topics with Your Partner**  
   If you are in a relationship, initiate open conversations about the topics covered in this book - legal, social, physical, financial, and emotional readiness.  

3. **Evaluate Your Readiness**  
   Assess your own readiness in the areas discussed. Are there aspects where you feel confident? Are there areas where you need more preparation or support?  

4. **Seek Guidance and Support**  
   Reach out to mentors, family members, or trusted advisors to discuss your thoughts and concerns. Their perspectives can provide clarity and encouragement.  

5. **Commit to Lifelong Learning**  
   Remember that marriage and family life are ongoing journeys of growth. Commit to learning, adapting, and nurturing your relationships with patience and intention.  

We are here to support you on this path. May your journey be filled with wisdom, love, and Grace.
