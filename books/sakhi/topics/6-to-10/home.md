## 6. Love marriage checklist

*   This guide advises couples in love marriages to check beyond just compatibility, considering family acceptance, cultural gaps, financial alignment, expressive love expectations, and the usefulness of premarital counseling.

---

## 7. Know Your Partner

*   This guide encourages those in arranged marriages to know their partner beyond the biodata by focusing on open communication, shared life goals, respecting boundaries, and emotional compatibility.

---

## 8. Financial Woes? Build Stability Before Family!

*   This guide advises building financial stability before starting a family, emphasizing financial planning, addressing debt stress, creating shared financial goals, and building a solid foundation together.

---

## 9. Victim of Abuse? Reclaim Your Life, Find Your Strength!

*   This guide offers support to victims of abuse, emphasizing that they are not alone, healing is possible, empowerment is key to breaking the cycle, and their voice matters in seeking justice.

---

## 10. Healers, Help Others Build Strong Marriages!

*   This guide encourages healers to help others build strong marriages by offering tools for emotional healing, guiding couples towards healthy communication, facilitating spiritual connection, and empowering families to thrive.
