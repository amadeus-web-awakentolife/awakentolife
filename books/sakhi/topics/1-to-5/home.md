## 1. Healthy unions

This guide advises parents to model healthy relationships, teach communication and respect, discuss values and expectations, and support their children's emotional development.

---

## 2. Emotional intelligence

This guide encourages students to build emotional intelligence before entering relationships by focusing on self-awareness, managing emotions effectively, developing empathy and compassion, and building strong communication skills.

---

## 3. Single and ready

This guide advises singles to prepare for a lasting union through self-reflection, healing past wounds, setting clear relationship intentions, and building a strong foundation for future love.

---

## 4. Anxiety about marriage

This guide suggests addressing fears and concerns, seeking pre-marital counseling, communicating openly with a partner, and building trust and security together to find peace of mind regarding anxiety about marriage.

---

## 5. Soul search

This guide encourages those dating to soul-search by considering their prompts for dating, shared values, unresolved past hurts, opinions about the gender they wish to date, how they decided on that gender, and the anticipated length of the dating period.
