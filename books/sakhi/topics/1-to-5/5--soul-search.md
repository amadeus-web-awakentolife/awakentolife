## Dating? Don't just swipe, soul-search!

* What are your prompts for dating? Why?
* Shared values, how will you ascertain that?
* Do you have unresolved past hurts that you are dealing with?
* What is your general opinion about the gender you wish to date?
* How did you decide if this was the gender you wished to date?
* How long do you anticipate the dating period to be
