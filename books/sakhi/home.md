




Sakhi is designed to serve as a comprehensive handbook for young people looking to make decisions about marriage and family life. With this intent, the author has drawn from her own marital experiences to highlight every aspect of marriage or separation and annulment to ensure that young adults, particularly girls, are fully informed of their rights and responsibilities.
The handbook outlines legal, physical, emotional, financial, and spiritual preparations, besides offering insights on conscious conception and early parenting.
Sakhi , the book aims to foster a generation of thoughtful individuals, whether they choose to share their lives with a partner or remain single.
