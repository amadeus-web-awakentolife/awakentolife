## 6. Emotional Readiness  

This chapter focuses on the emotional preparedness required for building and sustaining healthy relationships. It explores the psychological factors that influence relationships and provides practical guidance on improving self-esteem to foster emotional resilience and mutual understanding.  

---

### 6.1 Psychological Factors That Affect Relationships - How to Detect Them  
   Discusses common psychological factors, such as attachment styles, communication patterns, and unresolved past traumas, that can impact relationships. It provides insights into recognizing these factors in oneself and one's partner, along with strategies to address them constructively. The chapter emphasizes the importance of self-awareness and emotional intelligence in navigating relationship challenges.  

---

### 6.2 Self-Esteem Improvement  
   Highlights the role of self-esteem in building strong and healthy relationships. It offers practical tips for improving self-esteem, such as practicing self-compassion, setting boundaries, and celebrating personal achievements. The chapter also explores how a positive self-image contributes to emotional stability and enhances one's ability to connect with others authentically.
